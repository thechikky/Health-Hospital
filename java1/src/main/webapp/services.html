Our Services

  • Emergency Services:
    Emergency Room (ER) for urgent care and trauma.
    Ambulance services for emergency transportation.

  • Inpatient Services:
    General Medicine and Surgery.
    Intensive Care Units (ICUs) for critical care.
    Maternity and Neonatal Care.
    Pediatric Care.

  • Outpatient Services:
    Consultation with specialists.
    Minor surgical procedures.
    Diagnostic services (labs, imaging).
    Rehabilitation and physical therapy.

  • Surgical Services:
    General Surgery.
    Orthopedic Surgery.
    Cardiac Surgery.
    Neurosurgery.

  • Diagnostic Services:
    Laboratory tests (blood tests, biopsies).
    Imaging (X-rays, MRI, CT scans, ultrasound).
    Endoscopy and other diagnostic procedures.

  • Therapeutic Services:
    Physical Therapy.
    Occupational Therapy.
    Speech Therapy.
    Chemotherapy and Radiation Therapy for cancer patients.

  • Specialty Services:
    Cardiology.
    Oncology.
    Neurology.
    Gastroenterology.
    Pulmonology.
    Urology.

  • Maternity and Childbirth Services:
    Prenatal and postnatal care.
    Labor and delivery.
    Neonatal intensive care.

  • Mental Health Services:
    Psychiatric evaluations and treatment.
    Counseling and therapy.
    Substance abuse treatment programs.

  • Preventive and Wellness Services:
    Vaccinations and immunizations.
    Health screenings (mammograms, colonoscopies).
    Wellness programs (nutrition counseling, weight management).

  • Support Services:
    Social Work and Case Management.
    Palliative and Hospice Care.
    Patient Education and Support Groups.

  • Pharmacy Services:
    Dispensing medications.
    Medication counseling and management.
